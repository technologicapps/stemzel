Stemzel Support

Need help with imports, splitting, purchases, or exports? We’re here to help.

Contact

Email support@stemzel.com and include:

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version
  • Stemzel version (Settings → bottom of screen)
  • A short description of what happened

Restore your purchase

Open Stemzel → onboarding paywall → Restore Purchase. Make sure you’re signed into the same Apple ID you used to buy the app.

Import tips

  • File: Use MP3, M4A, WAV, or video files with an audio track.
  • URL: Paste a direct link to an audio file. Sharing pages (YouTube, Spotify, Google Drive, etc.) are not supported.
  • Record: Allow microphone access when prompted.

Privacy

Read our Privacy Policy. Stemzel processes audio entirely on your device.
