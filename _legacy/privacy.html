Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Stemzel (“we”, “the app”) is built to keep your music private. This policy explains what Stemzel does and does not do with your information.

Summary

  • All audio processing happens on your iPhone.
  • We do not upload your songs, recordings, or stems to our servers.
  • We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.
  • We do not require an account.

Information we do not collect

Stemzel does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, contacts, location, or browsing history.

Audio and files

When you import, record, or split audio, those files stay on your device unless you choose to export or share them using iOS system features (for example, saving to the Files app).

Microphone

If you use Record, Stemzel requests microphone access only while you are recording. Recordings are stored locally on your device.

Network use

If you import audio from a URL, Stemzel downloads that file directly to your device for processing. We do not receive a copy of that download.

Purchases

In-app purchases and restores are handled by Apple. We do not receive your payment card details. Apple may provide us with purchase status so we can unlock the app.

Data stored on your device

Stemzel stores your projects, purchase unlock status, and app preferences locally using iOS storage (including UserDefaults). You can delete projects from within the app.

Children

Stemzel is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will change when we do.

Contact

Questions? Email support@stemzel.com or visit Support.
